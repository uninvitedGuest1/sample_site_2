Where nature and wonder collide

Visit our island

Why visit here of all places?

The island is a hub for some of the rarest beasts seen in nature. From the most graceful to some of the most gore animals the planet has to offer.he balance between the truly wild and the mild makes your experience like none other that you have experienced. It is basically a paradise for animal lovers. For those that love bird watching, the island has been listed among the top 25 destinations for bird watchingwith more than 86 rare species of birds. Finally the island boasts over 67 unique and rare plant types to explore

Our journey to discover this wonderland

This place was discovered by 4 adventure seeking boys who wandered out into the sea hoping to travel the world on a patched up sail boat. Needless to say, we failed to travel the world on our boat, but got the world to travel to us. Since landing on the island, we've attracted over 30,000 unique visitors and we hope everyone visits this place at least once in their lifetime.

Traverse the jungle and discover new wildlife

Led by a team of tracking experts, you will journey through the thick forest to the pond that sits smack down at the center of the island, all the while exploring and uncovering animal and plant species you didn't even know existed.

The fullfilling sense of accomplishment and oneness with nature will give you plenty of ecstasy to last a full year. Our island prides itself in this jungle journey much more than anything else.
